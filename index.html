INFO & CONTROLS

HOW TO PLAY

You're dropped into an endless arena and the goal is simple: survive. Pick a weapon, then move with WASD (or the arrow keys), aim with your mouse, and hold or click to fire. Enemies pour in wave after wave, and each one you kill drops XP. Collect enough and you level up mid-run, which lets you pick a buff — more damage, faster attacks, extra defense, life steal, whatever fits your build. There's no single "right" choice; part of the fun is stacking buffs that turn your weapon into something ridiculous.

The longer you last, the nastier it gets. Tougher enemies and bosses show up as the waves climb, so keep moving, use your dash to slip out of bad spots, and don't get greedy chasing kills into a corner. When you eventually go down, the iCoins you earned that run are yours to keep — spend them in the iCoin Shop on permanent upgrades and unlocks that make your next run stronger. Bosses can even drop rare crates containing weapon skins. Die, upgrade, and come back harder. That's the loop.
